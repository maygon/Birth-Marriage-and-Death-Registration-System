About Us

A well-established civil registration system makes sure that all significant life events, such as births, marriages, and deaths, are recorded and issues the necessary certificates as evidence of such registration. The effective use of resources and help, as well as more precise tracking of progress toward the Sustainable Development Goals, are all facilitated by civil registration.

One of the most significant occurrences in Jamaicans' life is the registration of births, deaths, and marriages, which involves Mark's E-Registry (BDM). We put a lot of work into providing a helpful and understanding service. We take pride in processing and storing of data important information for our Jamaican people and each neighborhood.

The only repository for birth, death, marriage, and fetal death records in Jamaica is the Registrar General's Department (RGD), an Executive Agency. The RGD is the only agency in Jamaica tasked with keeping records of life-changing occasions such births, fetal deaths, marriages, and deaths.
